How Discover Works

Before the AI agent reads any source file, it asks the documentation first. Discover routes your query through the right layer and returns exactly what the agent needs.

The Search Cascade

When you search without a prefix, Discover cascades through five layers. It stops as soon as it finds results. The deeper it goes, the broader (and slower) the search.
